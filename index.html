About

PT. Nadira Permata Mandiri was established in 2014 and began operating as a freight forwarding and logistics company in 2020. We handle import and export of goods as well as inter-island shipping in Indonesia.

With expertise in LCL, FCL, general cargo, project cargo, and warehousing, we also serve a broad network of domestic and international clients covering freight forwarding, customs clearance, port handling, warehousing, and door-to-door logistics.
